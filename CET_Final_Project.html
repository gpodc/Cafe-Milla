Our Menu

Americano

A staple coffee type, a mixture of typically hot water and espresso. Perfect for people who loves a stronger coffee.

Vanilla Latte

A sweetened strong brewed coffee that is perfect for vanilla and sweets lover. Typically served as a cold brew.

Caramel Latte

A mixture of espresso along with caramel syrup and pouring steamed milk on top gives this latte a sweetened flavor.

Spanish Latte

A mixture of espresso-based drink with either normal milk or condensed milk or both. It is typically sweeter than normal lattes.

Our Love for Coffee

Café'millia got its beginnings as a group of friends' pastime. They were all oriented toward coffee and enjoyed brewing various variations of some of the most well-liked coffee varieties. They began modestly by developing their own version of popular brews and offering them to local residents. They gradually gained popularity to the point that they made the decision to grow by opening an online store in order to serve a bigger population with their coffee.
